Dedication

Design isn’t just about aesthetics; it’s about solving problems. Take a highlighter, for example. It’s simple, yet it makes information stand out. That’s what design should do—improve our world in a simple, efficient way.

That’s why I became a designer: to create experiences that are not only beautiful but deeply functional. My mission is to design solutions that resonate and make a meaningful impact.
